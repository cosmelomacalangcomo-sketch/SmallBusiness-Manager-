# BizTrack: Small Business Management System

A lightweight business management dashboard designed for small 
business owners to track inventory, record sales, and monitor 
business performance — all in one place.

## Features
- Dashboard with KPI cards: Revenue, Stock, Profit, Alerts
- Inventory management: add, remove, and search products
- Sales recorder with automatic total calculation
- Payment method tracking: Cash, GCash, Maya, Card
- Reports page with profit margin analysis per product
- Low stock and out-of-stock alerts

## Tech Stack
React.js | CSS3 | JavaScript (ES6+)

## Purpose
Inspired by real small business experience, this system helps 
Filipino entrepreneurs manage their products and sales 
digitally — reducing manual tracking and improving 
decision-making.
